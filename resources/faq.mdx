# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the indeFi platform, allocations, and security.

## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is indeFi?">
    indeFi.io is a web-based treasury management platform for web3-native capital. It enables users to allocate digital assets into professionally managed yield programs structured around duration-based lock-up periods.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is indeFi a decentralized application (dApp)?">
    No. indeFi is a centrally operated and managed platform that utilizes blockchain networks for settlement, asset transfers, and wallet authentication. It is not a dApp, DAO, permissionless protocol, or token issuance system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What assets does indeFi support?">
    The platform currently supports allocations using USDC and USDT. Support for additional digital assets, including ETH and BTC, may be introduced in future platform expansions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does indeFi have a token?">
    No. No governance token exists. No utility token exists. indeFi does not issue any form of cryptocurrency.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Allocations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do allocations work?">
    indeFi organizes allocations around duration-based lock-up periods rather than individual products. Users select a lock-up duration, deposit supported assets, and the platform manages the allocation through its treasury infrastructure.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the Gamma Vault?">
    The Gamma Vault is the platform's flagship allocation program. The platform is transitioning toward a broader duration-based structure over time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are returns guaranteed?">
    No. indeFi does not guarantee profits or fixed returns. All digital asset allocations involve risk, including possible loss of capital.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do withdrawals work?">
    Withdrawal availability depends on your allocation's lock-up duration, current liquidity conditions, and platform operational status. Specific procedures are outlined in your allocation terms.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is indeFi safe to use?">
    indeFi maintains a security-focused operational framework with layered safeguards. However, all digital asset activities involve risk. Users should review the security documentation and follow best practices.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why does indeFi need wallet access?">
    indeFi uses blockchain networks for settlement and authentication. Wallet connection is required for identity verification and to process deposits and withdrawals on-chain.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does indeFi use unlimited token approvals?">
    No. indeFi does not utilize unlimited token spending approvals, hidden wallet-draining logic, or malicious approval mechanisms.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I verify I'm on the real indeFi site?">
    Always verify you're on the official `indefi.io` domain, check for the SSL padlock icon, and review all wallet prompts carefully before signing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
