# Platform Overview & Security Whitepaper

**Version 1.1 — May 2026**

---

## 1. Executive Summary

[indefi.io](https://indefi.io) is a web-based treasury management platform for web3-native capital.

The platform enables users to allocate digital assets into professionally managed yield programs structured around duration-based lock-up periods rather than individual speculative products or decentralized protocol interaction.

inDeFi.io utilizes blockchain networks primarily as:

- Settlement infrastructure
- Asset transfer rails
- Wallet authentication systems

The platform itself is centrally operated and managed.

<Note>
  inDeFi.io is **not**: a decentralized application (dApp), a DAO, a permissionless protocol, an anonymous smart contract platform, or a token issuance system.
</Note>

The platform's objective is to provide users with streamlined access to professionally managed digital asset yield infrastructure while emphasizing:

- Treasury management
- Operational stability
- Liquidity management
- Infrastructure security

---

## 2. Platform Classification

inDeFi.io operates as a managed web platform utilizing blockchain settlement infrastructure.

Users interact with:

- A web-based application
- Managed allocation systems
- Treasury operations
- Internal risk-management infrastructure

The platform does not offer decentralized governance functionality or permissionless financial protocol interaction.

- **No governance token exists**
- **No DAO governance exists**
- **No autonomous user-directed smart contract interaction model exists**

---

## 3. Platform Structure & Allocation Model

The platform currently supports allocations using:

- **USDC**
- **USDT**

Support for additional digital assets, including ETH and BTC, may be introduced in future platform expansions.

Rather than presenting users with numerous individual products, the platform organizes allocations primarily around lock-up duration structures.

Longer-duration allocations may provide higher target yield opportunities due to increased capital stability and treasury planning efficiency.

<Warning>
  inDeFi.io does not guarantee profits or fixed returns. All digital asset allocations involve risk.
</Warning>

---

## 4. Allocation Infrastructure

The platform's flagship allocation program is currently referred to as the **Gamma Vault**.

Operationally, assets are managed through internal treasury segmentation frameworks, including categories such as:

- Treasury
- Operations
- Allocation Infrastructure

This structure is intended to improve:

- Liquidity oversight
- Operational accounting
- Treasury coordination
- Platform scalability

Users do not directly manage or control backend treasury operations.
