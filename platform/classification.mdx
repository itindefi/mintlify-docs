# Platform Classification

inDeFi.io operates as a managed web platform utilizing blockchain settlement infrastructure—not as a decentralized protocol.

## What inDeFi is NOT

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Not a Decentralized Application (dApp)">
    inDeFi is centrally operated with professional oversight. There is no permissionless interaction with autonomous smart contracts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Not a DAO">
    No decentralized autonomous organization governance exists. Platform decisions are made by the operational team.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Not a Permissionless Protocol">
    Users interact with managed systems, not permissionless financial protocols.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Not a Token Issuance System">
    inDeFi does not issue governance tokens, utility tokens, or any form of cryptocurrency.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
