## What is inDeFi?

inDeFi utilizes blockchain networks primarily as:

- **Settlement infrastructure** - Secure transaction finality
- **Asset transfer rails** - Efficient digital asset movement
- **Wallet authentication systems** - Cryptographic identity verification

The platform itself is centrally operated and managed with a focus on:

- Treasury management
- Operational stability
- Liquidity management
- Infrastructure security

<Note>
  inDeFi is **not** a decentralized application (dApp), DAO, permissionless protocol, anonymous smart contract platform, or token issuance system. It is a professionally managed platform utilizing blockchain infrastructure.
</Note>
