## Security Best Practices for Users

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify the URL">
    Always ensure you're on the official **indefi.io** domain before connecting your wallet
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Transaction Details">
    Review all transaction details in your wallet before signing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use Secure Devices">
    Access indeFi from secure, updated devices
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Use two-factor authentication on your wallet where available
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hardware Wallets">
    Consider using hardware wallets for significant allocations
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**Never share your private keys or seed phrase with anyone.** indeFi will never ask for this information.
</Warning>
