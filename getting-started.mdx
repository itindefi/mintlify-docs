# Getting Started

This guide will walk you through connecting to indeFi and making your first allocation.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Compatible Wallet">
    A Web3 wallet such as MetaMask, WalletConnect-compatible wallet, or Coinbase Wallet
  </Step>
  <Step title="Supported Assets">
    USDC or USDT on a supported blockchain network
  </Step>
  <Step title="Network Configuration">
    Your wallet configured for the appropriate network
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting Your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit indeFi">
    Navigate to [indefi.io](https://indefi.io) in your browser
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Wallet">
    Select the "Connect Wallet" button in the navigation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Wallet">
    Choose your preferred wallet provider from the available options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve Connection">
    Confirm the connection request in your wallet
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
indeFi will **never** ask you to:
- Share your private keys or seed phrase
- Approve unlimited token spending
- Sign transactions you don't understand

If prompted for any of these, you may be on a phishing site.
</Warning>

## Making an Allocation

Once connected, you can allocate your digital assets:

1. **Select Duration** - Choose your preferred lock-up period
2. **Enter Amount** - Specify the amount of USDC or USDT to allocate
3. **Review Terms** - Understand the allocation terms and expected timeline
4. **Confirm Transaction** - Sign the transaction in your wallet

## Understanding Lock-up Periods

indeFi organizes allocations around duration-based lock-up structures:

| Duration | Characteristics |
|----------|-----------------|
| Short-term | Higher liquidity, lower target yields |
| Medium-term | Balanced approach |
| Long-term | Lower liquidity, higher target yield opportunities |

<Note>
Longer-duration allocations may provide higher target yield opportunities due to increased capital stability and treasury planning efficiency.
</Note>
